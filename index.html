About us

Our Mission

As an integral part of the community led education and development venture (Valley Community Workspace) our goal is to promote bikes as a form of transport as well as recreation for both young and old. By helping cyclists with advice, tools, spares and hands on repairs from our enthusiastic volunteers, we aim to encourage cycling and similar forms of self-propelled locomotion for transport and recreation in our community. We accomplish our goal by firstly taking in unwanted bike, parts or accessories for reuse, recycle or resale. Then our volunteers rescue, repair and distribute the bikes, parts or accessories for use in the community minimising wasted and disposing of unusable materials responsibly. Our dedication to bicycles and assisting the community enables us to work while enjoying the company of other and having fun in a community setting.

What We Do

Our volunteers can fix just about anything on your bike from punctures to gear problems to bent wheels and more all done free of charge but donations would be appreciated. If you do not have a bike one Our volunteer mechanics will help fix up one of our donated, preloved bikes for you.

Donations

There are not set charge$ for what we do or provide, but we do have cost$ so to be sustainable we need your donation$ - just what you can afford. We also welcome volunteers to assist, experienced or not. There is plenty to do.
